# Demo Video Script (8-15 Minutes)

## Intro (0:00 - 1:30)

- **Visual**: Landing page / Login screen with futuristic animations.
- **Audio**: "Welcome to NEXUS ERP, the next-generation Real Estate Management System. Today, we're exploring a platform designed for 2025, featuring a glassmorphic UI, real-time updates, and seamless role-based workflows."
- **Action**: Log in as an **Admin**. Show the smooth transition to the Dashboard.

## Admin Dashboard & Analytics (1:30 - 3:00)

- **Visual**: Dashboard with glowing cards, charts, and activity feed.
- **Audio**: "Here is the Command Center. Admins get a bird's-eye view of the entire operation. Notice the real-time stats for Leads, Claims, and Revenue. The UI is designed to be immersive yet functional."
- **Action**: Hover over charts, scroll through recent activity.

## Leads Management (Kanban) (3:00 - 5:30)

- **Visual**: Leads Kanban board.
- **Audio**: "Let's look at our pipeline. This Kanban board is powered by dnd-kit for smooth drag-and-drop interactions. Operators can easily move leads through stages."
- **Action**: Drag a lead from 'New' to 'Contacted'. Click on a lead to show details (mock modal).
- **Audio**: "State updates are instantaneous and synced across the team."

## Properties & Clients (5:30 - 7:30)

- **Visual**: Products list and Clients table.
- **Audio**: "Managing inventory is effortless. Here we have our Real Estate assets. And here are our Clients. We can track every interaction and assigned property."
- **Action**: Navigate to Properties, then Clients. Show the clean table layouts.

## Client Portal & Claims (7:30 - 10:00)

- **Visual**: Log out, then log in as **Client**.
- **Audio**: "Now, let's switch gears. This is what your clients see. A personalized portal to view their purchased properties and submit claims."
- **Action**: Navigate to 'My Portal'. Submit a new claim (e.g., "Leaking Roof"). Use the drag-and-drop file upload.
- **Audio**: "The claim is submitted instantly. Operators are notified immediately."

## Code & Architecture (10:00 - 12:00)

- **Visual**: VS Code showing project structure.
- **Audio**: "Under the hood, we're running Next.js 14 with Hono for the backend API. We use Prisma for type-safe database interactions and Tailwind for this beautiful styling. The entire app is containerized with Docker for easy deployment."
